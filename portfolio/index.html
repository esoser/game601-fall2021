(Midterm) Non-Digital Game: A MOST HAUNTED HOUSE

In A Most Haunted House, players work together as members of a rag-tag team of paranormal investigators collecting proof that the house is haunted. If players are unable to collect enough Proof and reach the front door before overstaying their welcome, they are doomed to join the eternal residents of the house forever.

A Most Haunted House is a cooperative 4-player game. All players either win together or lose together.

The goal is for players to work together to collect 6 Proof cards as a team. The players lose if they are unable to reach the front door with 6 Proof cards in hand before 12 hours pass on the Clock.
